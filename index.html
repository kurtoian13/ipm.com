Get Started

How to use the iPhone 11 & 12 Pro Portrait mode

  • It’s simple to activate portrait mode!

1. OPEN CAMERA

nothing has changed from previous models here either. Simply swipe to the Portrait mode from the main camera screen.

2. FRAME PHOTO

Once activated, you’ll notice tips might appear on screen. For example, it might tell you to get closer to your subject - if you’re too far away it simply won’t work.

3. CHOSE SETTING & SHOOT!

When Portrait mode is ready, the selected “lighting effect” will turn yellow to let you know you’re good to go. There are lots of different Lighting Effects to choose from, but by default this will be set to “Natural Light”.

Do Your Reaserch

The more you know about your location, subject, and lighting, the better your photos will come out

TAKE ALOT OF PHOTOS

We live in the digital age, there is no excuse to miss the perfect shot. you can always delit and "edit down" to the best frames

SHARE YOUR PHOTOS

The more people that see your photos, the more feedback you get, and the more you can see what people like

FRAME IT

Make sure what you want in the frame, is actually inframe, then make sure the back ground and forground are clear of unwanted items

GO GET THE SHOT

Get out into the world and find differnt places, things, and angles! Taking photos of the same things hone skills, but go shoot somthing different

BRING A KIT

Make sure you have everything you need. Props, different clothes, or anthing else that may help add to the photo
